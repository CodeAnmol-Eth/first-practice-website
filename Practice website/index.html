About

Hrithik Roshan is an iconic Indian actor and producer born on January 10, 1974, in Mumbai, widely celebrated for his versatile roles and exceptional dancing skills. Over a career spanning more than two decades, he has established himself as one of Bollywood's highest-paid actors, earning six Filmfare Awards.
Early Life and BreakthroughBorn
into a prominent film family—his father is filmmaker Rakesh Roshan—Hrithik faced early personal struggles with a severe stutter and initially worked as an assistant director on his father's films. He catapulted to superstardom overnight with his leading debut in the blockbuster romance Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai (2000), a massive commercial success that earned him both Best Male Debut and Best Actor awards at the Filmfares.
Personal Life
He was previously married to interior designer Sussanne Khan from 2000 to 2014, and they share two sons, Hrehaan and Hridhaan.
